# 02 ETHOS AND PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

## Layer 1 Purpose

G-Codex currently operates as a Layer 1 system for one prime user.

Its purpose is practical:
- protect focus in day-to-day work
- preserve continuity across sessions and repos
- improve effective application-building execution
- support long-term resilience, family security, and economic usefulness through sustained technical capability

## Current Scope (Layer 1)

1. Single-prime-user operating model.
2. Local-first deterministic workflows.
3. Non-destructive proposal/review execution.
4. Repo-bound memory and truth anchors for continuity.

Layer 2 directions (multi-user expansion, broader sharing, commercialization) are valid future paths but are not the current design center.

## Product Principles

1. **Truth Over Optimism:** repository reality is checked first; assumptions yield to evidence.
2. **Focus Protection:** reduce context switching and startup friction.
3. **Continuity First:** preserve state, decisions, and active mission context.
4. **Deterministic Local-First Execution:** keep critical workflows reliable without hidden remote dependency.
5. **Application-Building Utility:** prioritize concrete shipping progress over abstract intelligence theater.
6. **Non-Destructive Governance:** proposals and external ideas are reviewed before implementation.
